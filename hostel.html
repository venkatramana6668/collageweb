Hostel



Hostels at IIIT Nuzvid cater to the pressing needs of students, addressing the increasing demands of contemporary life. The institute offers separate hostels for girls and boys, situated in different blocks, with dedicated wardens appointed on each floor to ensure the security of the residents and monitor entry and exit points. Strict regulations prohibit boys from entering the girl's hostel, maintaining a safe and secure environment. The hostel at IIIT Nuzvid fosters a friendly and harmonious atmosphere, where ragging is strictly banned, and seniors actively support and guide the newcomers. In addition, there are outsider faculties who serve as constant motivators, mentors, and even act as local guardians for the students

  • There are two floors in both & facilities like RO Water, Electricity, Cooler/A.C. is available 24*7.
  • Separate washrooms attached with every room .
  • Whole Hostel is Wi-Fi enabled so students are always updated by Google.
  • Accommodation facility for parents/guardians.

Medical Facilities

  • Twice a week a doctor comes for a regular check up.
  • One can avail first aid facility it aims to preserve life, prevent further harm and promote recovery.
  • 24×7 Ambulance facility for critical cases taken to city hospital.
  • An established dispensary in the college premises where all needed medicines are available 24×7.

Catering Facilities:

  • Hygienic food under the supervision of experts.
  • Variety of eatables in 4-courses (breakfast, lunch, refreshment, dinner)
  • Cleanliness is always kept into consideration.
  • Timings are feasible according to the students.
