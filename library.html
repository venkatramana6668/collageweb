Library



The IIIT Nuzvid library serves as a cornerstone of academic excellence within the institution. It plays a vital role in supporting the educational ethos of the institute by providing a comprehensive range of resources for students, faculty, and staff members. The fully-computerized library is equipped with state-of-the-art software, such as Alice for Windows 6.0 version and Delnet, ensuring efficient access to academic materials.

With a vast collection of over 50,000 books covering various engineering disciplines and applied sciences, the library offers students ample opportunities for in-depth research and learning. Additionally, it houses more than 5,000 CDs/DVDs (E-Books), providing digital resources that complement traditional printed materials. Subscriptions to over 60 international magazines, 40 national magazines, and eight newspapers further enrich the knowledge base available to the academic community.

The library's facilities also include a photocopying machine and a spacious seating capacity for over 300 students, promoting a conducive environment for focused study and exploration. By providing access to expensive and diverse resources, the library ensures that knowledge and education are not limited by financial constraints.

Overall, the IIIT Nuzvid library serves as a hub for disseminating knowledge and facilitating lifelong learning. It empowers students to delve into a multitude of subjects, fostering a spirit of intellectual growth and development within the institute.
