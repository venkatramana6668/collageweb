About Us

IIIT Nuzvid is its focus on providing quality education in the field of Information Technology (IT) and promoting excellence in IT education and research.!

About Us

The "Indian Institute of Information Technology, Nuzvid" (IIIT Nuzvid) was founded in 2017 to cater to the growing demand for quality education and research in the field of information technology. With a vision to provide a conducive learning environment and promote innovation, IIIT Nuzvid has quickly become a prominent institution in the country. The founders of IIIT Nuzvid were driven by the objective of nurturing talent and equipping students with the necessary skills to excel in the ever-changing world of technology. They aimed to create a hub of knowledge where students could not only gain a comprehensive education but also engage in cutting-edge research. Situated in the picturesque town of Nuzvid, IIIT Nuzvid enjoys a serene setting amidst natural beauty. The campus, surrounded by lush greenery and located near the Krishna River, offers an ideal atmosphere for academic pursuits. Students benefit from a peaceful environment that fosters creativity and intellectual growth. As part of the National Capital Region (NCR), IIIT Nuzvid contributes to the nation's economic growth and technological advancements. By leveraging its strategic location, the institute actively participates in shaping the digital landscape of the country.

The "Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Nuzvid" (RGUKTN) took on the responsibility of spreading knowledge and instilling values in Nuzvid, a city known for its green spaces and often referred to as "The Singhdwar of Andhra Pradesh." Nuzvid is a charming town nestled amidst the scenic Aravalli hills and the Sariska Tiger Sanctuary (National Park), offering its inhabitants a refreshing environment filled with clean, invigorating air. Furthermore, being part of the National Capital Region (NCR), it actively contributes to shaping the economic history of India.

Our Achievements

2019

IIIT Nuzvid received accreditation, recognizing its commitment to quality education. Faculty members secured research grants, advancing innovative research in various fields. IIIT Nuzvid students excelled in hackathons, showcasing their software development skills. Collaborative projects with industry partners fostered knowledge exchange and practical applications.

2020

IIIT Nuzvid established international collaborations, facilitating student exchanges and joint research. The campus infrastructure expanded, accommodating the institute's growing needs. Student-led startups emerged, gaining recognition and funding from venture capitalists. Industry tie-ups increased internship and placement opportunities for students.

2021

IIIT Nuzvid achieved high national rankings, reflecting academic excellence. Faculty and students published research papers, contributing to global knowledge. Technological innovations resulted in patents and awards for students. Student exchange programs provided diverse learning experiences.

2022

Faculty collaborated internationally, resulting in joint publications and advancements. IIIT Nuzvid students excelled in national-level examinations and received scholarships. Industry-sponsored labs provided state-of-the-art facilities for research projects. Alumni achieved success in their careers, contributing to the institute's growth.
